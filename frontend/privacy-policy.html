Arthamantri Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

Arthamantri is a research-oriented financial literacy and safety application. This policy explains what data we process, why we process it, and your choices.

2. Data We Process

  • SMS transaction signals (for debit/spend detection).
  • UPI app open signals and usage metadata (to trigger stage-2 safety warnings).
  • Notification metadata from payment apps (if notification access is granted).
  • Pilot feedback entries and consent records.
  • Basic app logs for reliability and research analytics.

3. Why We Use This Data

  • To estimate safe spending thresholds and trigger timely warning nudges.
  • To detect risky payment patterns and provide user-facing alerts.
  • To improve model/rule quality during pilot and research phases.

4. Permissions

  • SMS: identify bank debit events for overspending alerts.
  • Usage Access: detect UPI app open events.
  • Notification Access: parse payment-related notification signals.
  • Overlay: show USSD-style intervention warnings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers required to run the service.

6. Retention and Deletion

Pilot data is retained only as needed for research and system reliability. You can request deletion by contacting support.

7. Security

We use transport security (HTTPS) and operational controls to protect data in transit and during processing.

8. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact: support@arthamantri.app
